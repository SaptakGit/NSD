SCRIPT WRITER SENIOR GROUP

Application Fees : 1500/-

This category will have Online Submission only. This category is only open for the Senior Group (13 – 55 years). One application is eligible to send only one entry.

  1. Scriptwriters will have to send the script of one feature film. A detailed scripting/ screenplay of the first ten scenes and the last scene of the feature film along with the summary of the storyline are to be submitted for this purpose.
  2. The script has to be a detailed script. It should be a shooting script with visual treatment, shot division and detailed dialogues.
  3. The script should also include brief character sketches of the main characters.
  4. The script cannot be written using scriptwriting software.
  5. The script has to be written in a word document and submitted in PDF format.
  6. Our celebrity judges will select a specific number of scripts in each language (English, Hindi and other regional languages).
  7. These scripts will be used to make Netwood Originals.
  8. The selected Script writer will get prize money and the opportunity to write more scripts for Originals produced by Netwood.

The chart below shows the number of script writers to be selected, the prize money to be given out and the number of projects to be offered to the selected script writers.

DIRECTOR WITH TEAMSENIOR GROUP

Application Fees : 2200/-

This category will have Online Submission only. This category is only open for the Senior Group (13 – 55 years). One application is eligible to send only one entry.

  1. Each Director with her/ his team will have to send a short film of 20 minutes duration excluding the beginning and end titles. However, if the beginning and end titles are on the visuals of the cinematic narrative, then they would be included in the 20 minutes’ duration.
  2. The short film can be shot with any camera, (even a mobile camera would do), but the film has to be of HD quality. Also, the audio of the short film should not be of poor quality.
  3. In case the short film is in Hindi, Bengali or other regional languages, it should have subtitles in English.
  4. Our celebrity judges will select a specific number (see the chart below) of short films in each language (English, Hindi and other regional languages). These short films will be showcased on netwood.tv.
  5. The Directors with teams, whose entries get selected by our judges, will be informed about their selection by the month of April.
  6. The selected Directors with their teams have to come to the Grand Finale of the Netwood Talent Hunt, Season 1, to be held in Kolkata in the month of May to receive their rewards.
  7. The selected Directors with their teams will get prize money and the opportunity to direct more short films produced by Netwood.
